How It Works

Our proprietary software scours the web to identify high-quality products that offer the best value. We analyze specifications, user reviews, and price histories to curate a list of recommendations, saving you time and effort in your search for the best deals.

Affiliate Disclosure: When you click on a link to a product on our site and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps us maintain our service and continue finding the best products for our users.

Why We're Special

Our software, affectionately named PBU (Peanutbutter Unicorn), is like a good peanut butter sandwich: it's a simple, no-frills solution that just works. In a world of over-engineered and complicated tools, we focus on de-complicating the product discovery process and delivering solid, reliable value.

We achieve this by using a robust, JVM-based software stack built on the GraalVM with modern, efficient frameworks like Micronaut. This allows us to build a high-performance, low-overhead system that gets the job done right. We believe in transparency, and you can view the code that powers our service on my GitHub profile.
